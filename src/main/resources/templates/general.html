Luxury

Pension Moosholz offers a taste of luxury, nestled in Buntenbock in the Harz mountains. Our vision of this boutique guest house is simple, to provide guests with great service, facilities and surroundings. Ensuring your stay is special and filled with lasting memories is our priority.

Tasteful

Surrounded by mountains, lakes and woodland the Pension Moosholz, dating back to the early 18th century, has been tastefully restored and refurbished to 21st century standards. Each suite has a lounge, bedroom and en-suite bathroom and is furnished to provide our discerning guests with many special features.

Welcoming

You will receive a warm welcome at the Pension Moosholz whether you are looking for a quiet, romantic weekend retreat or a luxury home for a holiday to explore all that the Harz mountains have to offer. Our room rates include use of the Wellness area, tea and coffee in the room and a complementary bar in the lounge.
